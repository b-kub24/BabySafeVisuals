Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

TL;DR: BabySafeVisuals does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Period.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

BabySafeVisuals is designed with privacy at its core. We believe that your baby photos and family moments are precious and private. That's why we've built an app that works entirely on your device without sending any data to our servers or third parties.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. BabySafeVisuals does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store photos on our servers
  • Track your usage patterns
  • Access your location
  • Require account registration
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Photo Processing

All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device's built-in sharing features. We have no access to the photos you process with BabySafeVisuals.

Third-Party Services

BabySafeVisuals does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use third-party SDKs that collect data. The app is completely self-contained.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not track you. There are no analytics, no crash reporting services, and no behavioral tracking of any kind. We have no idea how you use the app, and we like it that way.

Children's Privacy

BabySafeVisuals is designed to help parents protect photos of their children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, there's no risk of children's data being collected.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data, there's nothing for us to secure on our end. Your photos remain on your device, protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometrics, etc.).

Your Rights

Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to access, correct, or delete from our servers. You have complete control over your photos at all times because they never leave your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date above. However, we are committed to maintaining a zero-data-collection approach.
