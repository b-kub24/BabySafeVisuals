Troubleshooting

The Parent Gate isn't working

Make sure you're pressing and holding the very top-right corner of the screen for a full 6 seconds. A progress indicator will appear. After 6 seconds, Face ID or Touch ID will prompt for confirmation.

The app crashes or freezes

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. For persistent issues, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

I purchased premium but scenes are still locked

Try using "Restore Purchases" in the Parent Menu (hold top-right 6 seconds). If you're using Family Sharing, make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID.
