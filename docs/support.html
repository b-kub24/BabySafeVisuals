  5. Save & Share - Save the protected photo or share it directly

Troubleshooting

The app isn't detecting faces correctly

Make sure the photo has good lighting and the faces are clearly visible. Very small faces or faces at extreme angles may not be detected.

The app crashes or freezes

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. For persistent issues, please contact support.

I can't access my photos

Make sure you've granted BabySafeVisuals permission to access your photo library. Go to Settings > Privacy > Photos and ensure BabySafeVisuals is enabled.
